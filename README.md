# 🇿🇼 Ethereum Community Hub - Harare (Built Different R&D Lab)

![Ethereum & AI](https://img.shields.io/badge/Ethereum-Community_Hub-627EEA?logo=ethereum&logoColor=white)
![Location](https://img.shields.io/badge/Location-Harare%2C%20Zimbabwe-00843D)
![Focus](https://img.shields.io/badge/Focus-Web3%20%2B%20AI-FF00FF)

The **Built Different Ethereum Community Hub and R&D Lab** is a grassroots initiative designed to bridge the gap between world-class technical talent in Zimbabwe and the global Ethereum ecosystem. Located in the heart of Harare, we are building a permanent home for co-working, deep technical learning, and agentic AI research.

---

## 🚀 Core Objectives

* **Represent Africa in the Ethereum Future:** Cultivating Zimbabwean builders to contribute to open-source protocols.
* **Direct Path to Income:** Transitioning developers from intense training to sustainably funded startups and international B2B contracting.
* **Convergent Tech:** Leading the intersection of Ethereum (Account Abstraction, ZK, Solidity) and Agentic AI.
* **Combatting Youth Unemployment:** Replicating and scaling the proven "Muzinda Hub" model for the decentralized era.

## 🏛️ Heritage & Track Record

Our mission is grounded in a deep history of tech community building in Zimbabwe:

* **Muzinda Hub Success:** Our core team helped train 1,000+ students and generated millions in digital job income.
* **Facebook Developer Circles:** leadership experience managing the largest active developer community in Harare for 4+ years.
* **Built Different (2020-Present):** Active Web3 ecosystem building with 300+ developers empowered across Harare and Kigali.

---

## 🎓 The Flagship Programme

We run a **6-Month R&D Pipeline** designed to create engineering excellence:

1. **Academic & Certification Sprint (Months 1-3):** Intensive training on EVM fundamentals, AI harnessing, and smart contract security (Cyfrin Updraft certification).
2. **Studio & Deployment Sprint (Months 4-6):** Transition to a functioning Web3 development agency, shipping live products to mainnets for real-world clients.
